Quarterly — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

Quarterly ("the app") helps you plan quarters, track habits, and review your progress. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it.

What we collect

Account details you provide: your name, email address, and timezone. Content you create: quarters, weekly goals, habits, habit completions, weekly reviews, and any feedback you send.

How your data is used and stored

Your data is used only to provide the app's features to you. It is stored in our PostgreSQL database (hosted on Supabase) and served by our API (hosted on Render). Passwords are stored only as salted hashes — we never see your password. We use an email provider solely to send verification and password-reset codes to your email.

What we do NOT do

We do not sell your data, show ads, or use third-party advertising or tracking. We do not share your data with third parties except the infrastructure providers above needed to run the service.

On-device data

The app can be used with no account — in that case your data is stored only on your device. When you sign in, your data syncs to the cloud so you can access it across devices.

Data retention and deletion

Your data is kept while your account is active. You can permanently delete your account and all its data at any time from Profile → Delete account in the app, or by following our account deletion instructions.

Children

The app is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their data.

Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected here with a new date.

Contact
