Delete your Quarterly account & data

Quarterly by Zentlify

You can permanently delete your Quarterly account and all associated data. This is immediate and cannot be undone.

Delete in the app (recommended)

  1. Open Quarterly.
  2. Go to the Profile tab.
  3. Tap Delete account.
  4. Confirm. Your account and data are deleted right away.

Or request deletion by email

Email support@zentlify.com from the address associated with your account, with the subject "Delete my account". We will delete your account and data within 7 days and confirm by email.

What gets deleted

  • Your account (name, email, timezone).
  • All your quarters, weekly goals, habits, habit completions, and weekly reviews.
  • Any feedback you submitted, and all sign-in tokens.

Nothing personal is retained after deletion.
